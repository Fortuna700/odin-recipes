Candied Yams

Description

This dish is made with tender yams or sweet potatoes, which are cooked until soft and then coated in a rich, syrupy glaze.

This dish is an all-time favorite during the holiday seasons.

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweet potatoes, drained
  • ¼ cup butter, cut into pieces
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 ½ cups miniature marshmallows
