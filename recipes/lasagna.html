Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic italian dish made out of meat, cheese, pasta, spices and seasonings, and of course, tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • lean ground meat
  • An Onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • An Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the following order: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
